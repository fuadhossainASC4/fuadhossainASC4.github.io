About Our App

DASH was created by a team of three friends whose aim was to eliminate the nuisance of waiting in line at the grocery store. The problem was approached by taking a smartphone (which 77% of Americans own) and turning it into a scanner. Allowing customers to scan items as they go. Essentially, the app allows users to grab and pay for items as conveniently and efficently as possible.

Information Cards

Information cards can be easily be accessed by swiping down after scanning a item. This allows users to find all the nutional details, ingredients, etc.

Allergy Checker

We know how diffcult it is to shop for something new, due to fear that it could harm you or your family. So we implemeted Allergy Checker. Setting it up is easy, all you have to do is select all the allergens that affect you, and when ever a item is scanned that contains the affecting allergen. A pop-up comes up warning the user.

Budget Watcher

Dash doesn't only want to save your time, but your money as well so we added a Budget Watcher. You simply input your weekly or monthly grocery budget and whever you exceed it during a shopping trip it alerts you and even suggets you to drop something unnecessary like a bag of chips.

Grocery List

As constant Dash users we understand that our users seek the most convenient experience. So we decided to implement Grocery List. This feature allows users to make a checklist of all the items before making the trip to the store. In addition it pre-calculates the total of your list, making sure you never run into the store with the correct change.

Self-Filling Pantry

Our advanced algorithm takes your most bought items and gives you the ability to automatically order it every other week or set amount of item of your preference. For example, if you have a favorite toilet paper, we will make it our responbility to order it every week. You also have the option to have it delivered to your doorsetp with Dash Delivery or Dash Pick-Up.

Dash Pick-Up

We understand that users don’t always have the time to go through the store to find their groceries or simply have a super busy schedule, so we created Dash Pick-Up. This allows users to order items and pick it up from the Dash Lane at a convenient time, for free*. In addition we furthered the level of convenience by bringing you another feature that's still a work in progress, Dash Delivery.This feature brings the grocery store right to your doorstep at a reasonable price point.

How The App Works?

Scan Items

Hold the camera over any barcode to add desired items to the shopping cart or to find more information about the product or good. Also if you can't find a certain item, you can search for it straight from the app. What about fruits and vegtables? No need to worry, they can still be scanned and brought to the DASH Lane to be weighed and added to your shopping cart.

Payment Options

Believe it or not, but all your items could be paid right from your phone. All you have to do is connect your mobile bank or any of the other form of payment we accept. And if you perfer going old-fashion, no problem. We accept cash at the DASH Lane.

Dash Lane

The DASH Lane differs from the other lanes becuase it can only be used by customers that use the app. This will allow the lane to be more efficenty, since all the customers have there items pre-scanned. Essentially, the DASH Lane is the "EZ-Pass" for grocery stores.

Check-Out / Security

Users who pay through the app will receive a E-recepit and can carry on with their busy lives. Checking-out with cash is just as simple. Only difference is that users would have to show the cashier the custom QR code generated for your shopping list and pay the total. Check-out has never been simpler. To ensure secuirty against a unscanned item, item scanners will be placed near the exists so if a unscanned item pass it will beep and draw attention. Much like the secuirty measures seen at clothing stores.
